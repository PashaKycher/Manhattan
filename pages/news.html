    New Seasonal Menu
    We are excited to introduce our new seasonal menu, featuring fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From vibrant salads to hearty mains, every dish is crafted to celebrate the flavors of the season.     Discover the art of pairing food and wine at our upcoming Wine Tasting Evening. Our sommelier has selected premium wines from around the world, perfectly matched with gourmet appetizers.     Every Friday night, our restaurant transforms into a vibrant venue with live music performances. Enjoy your favorite dishes while talented local artists create the perfect atmosphere.
